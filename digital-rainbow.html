Digital Rainbow (Fixed Audio Media)

Length: 8’24”

Date of Composition: 2009

This piece explores musical material based on FM synthesis via waveshaping. Contrast in between harmonic and inharmonic spectra, as well as sound granulation defined the musical universe that resulted. Signal processing was designed by visual programming Pure Data patches (Extended Version 0.39).

The piece is presented along with a visual score made by drawings and symbols based on Stéphan Roy's functional analysis. The international premiere was at FILE (Sao Paulo, Brasil) in 2012.
