As follows some graphic and sound excerpts illustrate the production of promotional CD’s and DVD’s lead and sponsored by the Cartagena Music Festival, released in between 2011-2013.

Go to the Cartagena Music Festival website here.

1. The Best Of The Festival: The Sound Of The Americas, Various artists

Salvi Foundation - Cartagena International Music Festival, Sixth Edition (2012).

"The Sound of the Americas" CD is a music compilation presenting the best of the sixth Festival edition. It features music from composers such as Golijov, Guarneri, Franck, Villa-Lobos, Ravel, Richard Galliano, between others. It includes performances from the Sao Paulo Symphony Orchestra, violinist Alexis Cardenas and his quartet, Steven Copes, Horacio Franco, Brian Ganz and the Saint Lawrence String Quartet. During the Festival, italian recording engineers Angelo Cioffi, Stephane Melone and Davide Ianuzzi were responsible for the overall recording of the performances. Recordings were mixed and mastered at Egea Records Santa Cecilia Auditorium (Perugia, Italy). The record was officially presented to the public at a chamber concert organized by the Foundation, at the Luis Angel Arango Library Concert Hall in august 2012.

Number of tracks: 14. Total length: 71 min.

Recording Engineering: Angelo Cioffi, Stephano Melone, Davide Ianuzzi

Mixing & Mastering: Angelo Cioffi (Santa Cecilia Auditorium, Perugia, Italy)

Design: Juliana Díaz

Photo: Fabian Acosta, Federico Ríos

Executive Production: Sergio Granada-Moreno

2. The Italian Style DVD Set, Various artists

Salvi Foundation - Cartagena International Music Festival, Seventh Edition (March, 2013).

RCN T.V. - TV Production & Broadcast.

"The Italian Style" set of DVD's is a compilation of the best performances presented during the seventh edition of the Cartagena Music Festival. It features artists such as Mario Brunello, Rinaldo Alessandrini and Salvatore Accardo and composers such as Monteverdi, Rossini, Vivaldi, Paganini and Scarlatti. Colombian private television network RCN T.V. was in charge of TV production and broadcast.

Number of DVDs: 10. Total length: 12 - 13 hours.

Recording Engineering: Marco Silva Zapata, Angelo Cioffi, Stephano Melone.

Audio Mixing for Television: Marco Silva Zapata.

Sound Editing & Mix Coaching: Sergio Granada-Moreno.

TV Broadcast Executive Production: Ricardo Bermudez.

Design: Image ID.

Executive Production: Sergio Granada-Moreno
